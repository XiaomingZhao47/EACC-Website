Top News Here

1. Our food giveaway has been helping individuals in the Easton Area for months thanks to Giant Food Store. Stop by every other thursday at 1:00 - schedule of dates in on the slide below.

Also come attend our Silver Connections Senior Program and participate in arts, music, senior fitness like chair yoga, educational lectures and quarterly field trips.

Watch our slide show to see the exciting events that the after school program did for Black History Month. Now we are on to International Womens Month. Thank you Ms. Maria, Ms. Mayche and Chef Derrick for your leadership as always.

2. Our Saints Clubhouse afterschool program is a great program for youth from K thru 8 grades. We offer homework help, engaging and innovative arts, music, learning experiences and physical fitness programs in our wonderful gym. Our lower level is our Cops n Kids room which is a phenomenal library and a place to come and pickup books or just relax and read with your children.

Big Easy Easton Jazz Band continues to provide music to after school children and teaches them how to play musical instruments. We absolutely adore Jeremy and his jazz band.
