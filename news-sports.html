AARP Tax-Aide Program Update

The AARP Tax-Aide program will not offer in person tax preparation services this year. However, they are making alternate arrangements.

EACC is currently a scan site. This means you will be able to drop off your documents for AARP to scan and review. AARP will then contact you for a virtual tax preparation meeting.
